TWS earphones have been extremely hot across almost all e-commerce platforms over the past 12 months but there are signs that this overheating frenzy is gradually cooling off, as incompetent manufacturers are gradually weeded out of the market due to fierce competitions. For instance, one could easily see 10,000+ different SKUs from 1000+ suppliers in 8 Exhibition Halls (Hall 1-4 for cross border e-commerce and Hall 13-16 for mobile consumer electronics) during Shenzhen Gift Show April 2021, but can only find roughly a mere fraction of those figures in Hall 14 only during the Oct session. Many bad manufacturers have probably died out or closing down. Yet again, there are still some factories are struggling for survival, feeding the market with cheap and problematic TWS earphones. 

In the last two months or so, we have been visiting dozens of manufacturers in the Big Bay Area, trying to identify suitable suppliers, which are capable and competent in offering quality products for brand OEMs.  We tested quite a number of TWS earphones and found many of them are with imperfections of this and that. 

Well, how to select a good pair of TWS earphone for your own private label and at the same time feel comfortable about it, not fearing that the bad quality would kill your brand overnight? Following are some of the main areas that you would have to test to examine carefully.
