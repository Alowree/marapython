Zu Ti and Liu Kun were from the Eastern Jin Dynasty. They were both diligent and ambitious young men.

东晋时期，有一对好朋友叫祖逖和刘坤。他们都是非常勤奋并野心勃勃的年轻人。

However, they felt their knowledge was not enough to serve their country. So they put all their energy into study.

然而，他们觉得自己的学识并不足够以报效祖国。于是他们将全部精力都投入到了学习中。

Once Zu Ti in the middle of the night heard roosters crowing in his sleep.

有一天半夜，祖逖在睡梦中听到公鸡在打鸣。

He woke Liu and said to him: “people say it is unlucky to hear roosters crowing in the middle of the night but I don’t think so. How about we get up and practice sword the moment we hear the rooster crowing from now on?”

他把刘坤踹醒说道： “人家说半夜听到鸡叫不吉利，但我不觉得。要不以后咱们听见鸡叫就起来练剑怎么样？

Liu Kun agreed. Every day they woke up at cockcrow and practiced the sword. And they never stopped. 

刘坤开心的同意了。每天他们伴着鸡叫起床练剑。冬去春来，从未停歇。

After a long period of study and training, Zu Ti grew into a general and Liu Kun a governor.

在经过漫长的学习与练习之后，祖逖成为了将军，刘坤也成为重臣。

Because of their story, people use 闻鸡起舞 to describe someone of noble aspirations **exerts himself** in time. 

正因他们的故事，人们用“闻鸡起舞”来形容那些有崇高志向的人，以及他们的勤奋和自律。
