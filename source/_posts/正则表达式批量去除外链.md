当我们使用Markdown进行写作时，有时候会从某些外部的网站上复制一些文本，而这些复制来的文本内有时有含有大量的外部链接，例如下面这段文本。

> Antarctica.
>
> An [inhospitable](https://www.definitions.net/definition/inhospitable) wasteland.
>
> But even here,
>
> on the Earth's [frozen](https://www.definitions.net/definition/frozen) bottom,
>
> we find life.
>
> And not just any life.
>
> Penguins.
>
> Joyous, frolicking, waddling,
>
> cute and [cuddly](https://www.definitions.net/definition/cuddly) life.
>
> Look at them tumbling
>
> onto [their](https://www.definitions.net/definition/their) [chubby](https://www.definitions.net/definition/chubby) bum-bums.
>
> Who [could](https://www.definitions.net/definition/could) take
>
> these [frisky](https://www.definitions.net/definition/frisky) [little](https://www.definitions.net/definition/little) snow clowns...
>
> Seriously? Does [anyone](https://www.definitions.net/definition/anyone) even know
>
> where we're [marching](https://www.definitions.net/definition/marching) to?
>
> \- Who cares?

好了，现在我们想要去除全部的外链，把文本改造成下面这样。

> Antarctica.
>
> An inhospitable wasteland.
>
> But even here,
>
> on the Earth's frozen bottom,
>
> we find life.
>
> And not just any life.
>
> Penguins.
>
> Joyous, frolicking, waddling,	
>
> cute and cuddly life.	
>
> Look at them tumbling
>
> onto their chubby bum-bums.
>
> Who could take
>
> these frisky little snow clowns...
>
> Seriously? Does anyone even know
>
> where we're marching to?
>
> \- Who cares?

鉴于Typora对正则查找和替换的支持不是特别好，改用Sublime Text 3来进行正则查找和替换。使用Sublime Text 3打开.md文件，按<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>H</kbd>组合键调出查找和替换的输入框，在Find和Replace栏位分别输入以下正则表达式：

Find: `\[(.*?)\]\(.*?\)`

Replace: `\1`

然后点击`Replace All`按钮，即可完成转换。

解析：这里使用正则表达式，在目标文本里查找所有格式为`[陌生单词](超链接)`的文本内容，然后使用[反向引用](https://www.marapython.com/posts/regular-expressions/#反向引用)将其一一替换为格式为`陌生单词`的文本内容。
